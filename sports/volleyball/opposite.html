The pipe attack from Zone 6 is an explosive approach from deep in the court. The longer run-up generates higher approach velocity, creating greater Achilles loading at takeoff than any front-row attack.

Pre-Match Routine

Opposite Hitter Achilles Activation (10 Minutes)

Perform before every practice and match. Designed for the right-side asymmetric demands and serve power of the opposite position.

1

Single-leg calf raise √ó 15 reps per leg. Compare ease and height between sides. If the right side feels weaker, add an extra 5 reps right-side before proceeding. This asymmetry awareness is the opposite's primary prevention habit.

Ankle circles 30 sec per side. Then right-ankle-specific dorsiflexion stretching for an additional 30 sec. Right-side approach demands greater right ankle dorsiflexion than other positions.

Jump-serve run-up at 60% effort √ó 5 reps, then 80% √ó 5 reps, then 3 near-full-effort serves. Build the Achilles into maximum-effort serve output progressively rather than cold.
